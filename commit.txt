[NSP] Add initial rank-2 support via tile_shape in MaterializeTile

Replace tile_size with tile_shape in nsp.materialize_tile to enable
multi-dimensional tiles (initially rank-2).

Update verification to ensure:

rank consistency between source, destination, and tile_shape
positive tile dimensions
compatibility with source shape
tile bounds fit within destination (when static)

This enables correct materialization of 2D elementwise workloads (e.g.
vector_add_2d) under the existing 1D sharding model (split on dim0).

Follow-ups will generalize fast-path lowering and N-D support further.
