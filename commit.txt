[NSP] Recognize destination subview materialization pattern

Add helper to recognize and materialize tensor results directly
into destination subviews via bufferization.materialize_in_destination.

Enables proper handling of the NSP Localize → Materialize contract
and avoids unnecessary tensor.empty in recognized cases.

[NSP] Model materialize_tile as a memory-effecting anchor op

Make nsp.materialize_tile implement MemoryEffectOpInterface and model it
conservatively as reading the source tensor and reading/writing the
destination memref.

This preserves the local compute/materialization contract across
canonicalization before NSPMaterializePass runs.
